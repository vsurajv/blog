Can you play Pokémon GO without paying? 

Published 3 months ago

It's understandable why the game Pokémon GO has become so popular. There is nothing else like this game in terms of immersion. You may go with your phone and capture Pokémon in real life in addition to playing it anywhere. It's a game that really shows off what technology is capable of, and it goes beyond just wandering the real world in search of Pikachu.

How to play Pokemon Go?

However, you undoubtedly have the following question on your mind: Can I play Pokémon GO for free? In that case, how? Yes, it is the answer. Actually, playing Pokémon GO for free is quite simple.

Here are some steps for doing it:
  1. Download and install an APK file into your phone or tablet from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. At this stage, you must be using an Android or iOS device (sorry, iPhone owners).
  2. Launch the app and sign into your account (there is no fee).
  3. After signing in, begin playing!

Is Pokemon Go still free?

The game enables in-app purchases for extra in-game stuff and is available for free to play. It operates on a freemium business model with local advertising. Initially, there were about 150 Pokémon species in the game; by 2021, there were about 700.
